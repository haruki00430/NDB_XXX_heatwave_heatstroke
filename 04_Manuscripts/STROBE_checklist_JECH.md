## Notes for Submission

1. **Item 1a**: The study design ("ecological study") appears in the Abstract (Methods) and in Keywords, not in the main title. This satisfies the STROBE requirement ("in the title or the abstract").

2. **Item 9 / 12c**: Missing data statement: all 47 prefectures had complete data for the outcome and primary exposure. Air conditioning prevalence (2014 survey) is complete but temporally mismatched — this limitation is disclosed in the Methods and Discussion.

3. **Item 16a**: Due to substantial collinearity (VIF >10), multivariable adjustment was not performed. Unadjusted estimates are reported with explicit rationale. This is acknowledged as a study limitation.

4. **Ecological design note**: Standard STROBE was developed for individual-level studies. For ecological (aggregate-level) studies, the unit of analysis is the prefecture rather than the individual. Where STROBE items reference "individuals," they apply here to prefectures. This is consistent with STROBE guidance for ecological adaptations.

5. **Page numbers**: Page numbers refer to the blinded manuscript submitted as the main document (manuscript_main.docx). Abstract and Key Messages: p. 1; Introduction: p. 2; Methods: pp. 2–3; Results: pp. 3–5; Discussion: pp. 5–6; References: pp. 6–7; Tables: pp. 7–8.
